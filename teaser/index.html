Vehicle Cost Calculator

Fuel is only the beginning.

A standalone vehicle cost experience is on the way. It is being rebuilt to help drivers see the full picture: real operating costs, aging tires, service timing, depreciation, financing pressure, and what all of that means for everyday driving, gig work, and long trips.

What is coming: a sharper, easier-to-share tool that turns year / make / model data into a realistic starting point, then lets you pressure-test the numbers against how you actually drive.
